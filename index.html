ANQUAN CAPITAL

Anquan is a new type of technology company, coupling original computer science research with advanced software engineering, to create secure, high velocity platforms for financial markets.

TECHNOLOGIES UNDER MANAGEMENT

We have taken pioneering research from the School of Computing at the National University of Singapore and have built

  • One of the world’s first scalable Distributed Ledger Technology platforms – unlike other DLT protocols, our technology is built for scalability both in terms of the network and transaction processing rate.
  • A hardware rooted secure platform – we can build micro containers that use commodity hardware to provide guaranteed safety in financial transactions

The security guarantees we provide with our platform are significantly stronger than prevalent solutions. The Anquan platform is based on hardware-rooted security and pre-supposes large-scale coordinated attacks on the infrastructure. We support real-world applications that require churn in network participants. The Anquan platform features a highly scalable consensus protocol that delivers transaction throughput growing almost linearly with the growth of the network size. Our initial research outcomes have received numerous accolades and spawned discussions at top scientific venues and cryptocurrency forums.

POWERING FINANCIAL MARKETS

We build applications using our platforms that have the potential to transform the way that entire financial markets or eco-systems work. We foster collaboration between market participants through trusted computing and are currently working with some of the world’s most innovative financial institutions in the following markets:

  • Fund management - long only and alternative
  • Investment banking - trading and settlement
  • Transaction banking - secure communications
  • Securities exchanges - OTC securities

We begin by working with a select group of FIs in a marketplace and innovate a use case for that market.

We build a POC based using our platform and test with the market participants.

Once we have thoroughly tested the POC, we deploy our platform technology across the marketplace.
