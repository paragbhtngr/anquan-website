TECHNOLOGIES & PLATFORMS

FROM LAB TO MARKET


We operate at the very leading edge, combining deep research from some of the world’s top research computer scientists together with industrial strength software built by world-class engineers.

Our technologies include:

  • One of the world’s first scalable Distributed Ledger Technology platforms – unlike other DLT protocols, our technology is built for scalability both in terms of the network and transaction processing rate.
  • A hardware rooted secure platform – we can build micro containers that use commodity hardware to provide guaranteed safety in financial transactions

The Anquan platforms feature a highly scalable consensus protocol that delivers transaction throughput growing almost linearly with the growth of the network size. Our research outcomes have received numerous accolades and spawned discussions at top scientific venues and cryptocurrency forums.
